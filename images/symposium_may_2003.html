Chicago History Project
Chicago Public Schools in partnership with the Chicago Historical Society, Chicago Metro History Education Center, Constitutional Rights Foundation Chicago, the Newberry Library, and the University of Illinois at Chicago.

Reading Assignment
1.  Eric Foner, Story of American Freedom
  • Introduction, pp. xiii-xxii.
  • Chapter 1, "The Birth of American Freedom," pp. 3-28.
  • One other chapter of interest to you.
2.  Your American History Textbook
  • Review the chapter corresponding to the chapter you chose in Foner's Story of American Freedom (for instance if you chose to read Chapter 6 in Foner you should review the textbook chapter on the Gilded Age).
Writing Assignment (please type a 1-2 page response)
  • Both Foner and your textbook are surveys of American history.  Obviously, each book has different goals and audiences.  Beyond those differences, how do these two surveys differ in their approach to and interpretation of American history? 
Be Prepared to Discuss:
  • Which historical topic is easiest for you to teach?  Which is most difficult?  Why the differences?
