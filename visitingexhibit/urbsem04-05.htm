This is a monthly workshop in which graduate students studying urban history issues present to their peers works-in-progress from their dissertations. Papers are pre-circulated by e-mail and must be requested in advance. If you are interested in presenting or would like to attend, please contact Christopher Miller christopher.miller@marquette.edu or John McCarthy john.mccarthy@marquette.edu.

The group is open only to graduate students (no faculty), and members should be committed to attending as many of the meetings as possible.
